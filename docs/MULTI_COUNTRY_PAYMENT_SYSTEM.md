# MULTI COUNTRY PAYMENT SYSTEM

## Introduction
This document provides an overview of multi-country payment systems, detailing international payment methods, regional compliance requirements, and currency handling practices.

## International Payment Methods
- **Credit Cards**: Widely accepted globally; offers buyer protection but has transaction fees.
- **Digital Wallets** (e.g., PayPal, Google Pay): Convenient for users; may not be accepted in all regions.
- **Bank Transfers**: Reliable for larger transactions; may take longer to process.

## Regional Compliance
- **North America**: Must comply with PCI-DSS standards for credit card processing.
- **Europe**: Follow GDPR for data protection; ensure local payment regulations are met.
- **Asia**: Be aware of regional regulations, which vary significantly between countries.

## Currency Handling
- Implement multi-currency support to cater to international users.
- Regularly update conversion rates to handle currency fluctuations effectively.

## Best Practices
- Ensure robust security measures to protect user data.
- Optimize user experience by providing clear payment options and support for various methods.
