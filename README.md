## Upload a photo to your agent profile in Google AI Studio

I can’t directly click through Google AI Studio from this environment, but here are the exact steps:

1. Open **Google AI Studio**: https://aistudio.google.com/
2. Sign in with your Google account.
3. Open your **profile/account** menu (top-right corner).
4. Go to **Profile settings** (or account settings, depending on UI version).
5. Click the **photo/avatar** area.
6. Choose **Upload photo** and select your image.
7. Crop/adjust if prompted, then click **Save**.
